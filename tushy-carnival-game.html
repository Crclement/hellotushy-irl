TUSHY Carnival Game

3-Player "Poop Race" — Bill of Materials

🎯 The Concept

Players spray their Tushy bidet at a target (bullseye/butt-shaped opening in the backboard). Behind each target is a funnel that catches the water stream and channels it through a flow sensor.

The Raspberry Pi reads each flow sensor in real-time — the more water hitting the target, the faster that player's poop emoji races along the track above. First emoji to reach the finish line wins!

🏁

Race Mode

Classic race — first to finish wins. Stepper motors move poop emojis proportionally.

💯

Points Mode

Timed round (30-60 sec). Flow sensors track total water volume per player. High score wins.

🎮

Target Practice

Multiple targets light up sequentially. Hit the lit target to score. RPi controls LEDs.

DESIGN NOTES

🔧 Industrial Mounting Design

Each bidet mounts to a 12"×14" Baltic birch plywood base plate. The base plate sits on a 6" heavy-duty lazy susan turntable bearing (500lb capacity) for smooth 360° pan rotation. An adjustable friction hinge bracket provides ~45° tilt with tension control.

The assembly mounts to a 12" schedule 40 black steel pipe riser (1-1/4") screwed into a floor flange bolted to the game counter. Total height: ~16" above counter. Rubber grip tape on the base plate edges gives players a solid handle for aiming. The industrial swivel + friction tilt system is more durable and easier to maintain than camera ball heads.

🚿 Plumbing Notes

Water supply: standard outdoor garden hose spigot → 25ft main hose → 3-way brass splitter. Each player gets a 10ft hose from the splitter → 3/4" GHT-to-3/8" compression adapter.

The adapter connects to the included Tushy 24" braided hose, which plugs into the bidet unit. The Tushy pressure control knob acts as the player's trigger — turn it to spray, turn back to stop.

⚠ Things to Watch Out For

  • Water Pressure: The Tushy knob is designed for indoor plumbing (~50 PSI). Use the included pressure regulator to keep it consistent.
  • Splash Zone: You WILL need tarps/barriers for bystanders. Plan for a 10ft splash radius.
  • Hose Routing: Run hoses underneath the counter through grommeted holes to keep them hidden and tangle-free.
  • Friction Tilt Adjustment: Pre-set the friction hinges before the event. Test with players of different strengths.
  • Drainage: The target board will get very wet. Build a drip tray / gutter at the base and route to buckets.
  • Bidet Nozzle Angle: The Tushy nozzle points up and back (designed to clean a butt). Mount the bidet with the nozzle facing FORWARD — flip it 180° on the base plate.

✨ Additions Worth Considering

  • Pressure regulator (~$10) at the spigot for consistent water pressure
  • Prize bell or horn for the winner — classic carnival touch
  • Scoreboard display (small monitor + RPi HDMI) showing race progress
  • Carnival-style signage and bunting to complete the look
  • Backup bidet(s) in case one gets damaged during the event

💪 Strengths of This Concept

  • The toilet-shaped prop mounts are perfect — immediately communicates the joke
  • The Tushy knob as the "trigger" is genius — it's the actual product being used as intended (sort of)
  • Poop emoji racers are maximally on-brand and universally recognized
  • The bidet itself is only 2 lbs, so the pan/tilt mechanism doesn't need to be heavy duty
